Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does one treatment session last? ▾
Usually one session lasts about 1 hour. In more severe or complex cases, it may extend to 1.5 or 2 hours. The duration depends entirely on the patient's condition and is decided during treatment. Charges remain the same regardless of duration.
Do you use needles for acupuncture? ▾
We offer both classic needle acupuncture and needle-free acupressure options. For patients uncomfortable with needles, our Acupressure Pressure-less technique delivers excellent results without any needles or discomfort.
Is the treatment safe? Are there any side effects? ▾
Yes, all our treatments are completely non-invasive and safe. We use US FDA-approved equipment and evidence-based techniques. Most patients experience zero side effects. Our approach focuses on natural healing without drugs or surgery.
Do you offer home visits? ▾
Yes! We offer home visits in Gurugram and nearby areas. If you or a family member is unable to travel to the clinic, our senior therapists can visit your home. Please call us on +91 93116 42999 or +91 70500 02000 to arrange.
Are you open on Sundays and public holidays? ▾
Yes! Healthy Being Clinic is open 7 days a week including Sundays and government/festival holidays. We understand health doesn't follow a calendar, so we are always available for you.
Can you really help avoid surgery? ▾
Many of our patients have successfully avoided surgery for knee/hip replacement, spine surgery, and ENT procedures through our holistic treatment approach. However, each case is evaluated individually. We are transparent about what we can and cannot help with — your wellbeing always comes first.
How do I book an appointment? ▾
You can book an appointment online via our booking form, call us directly at +91 93116 42999 or +91 70500 02000, or message us on WhatsApp. We are also on Instagram @biophysiotherapy and Facebook (HealthyBeing bio PhysioTherapy).
