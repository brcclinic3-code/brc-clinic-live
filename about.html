Dr. Arpita Shefali is a trailblazer in integrative and holistic medicine, combining the best of naturopathy, acupuncture, neurotherapy, physiotherapy and advanced diagnostics to deliver transformational results for her patients.

With over 15 years of hands-on clinical experience and 25,550+ patients treated, she has pioneered a unique approach that identifies the root cause of chronic diseases and addresses them through a synergistic blend of ancient healing wisdom and cutting-edge medical technology.

Her clinic is equipped with US FDA-approved medical devices sourced from Germany, Japan, China and India, enabling precise, non-invasive diagnosis and treatment for a wide spectrum of conditions — from spine disorders to fertility challenges, from neurological diseases to lifestyle disorders.
