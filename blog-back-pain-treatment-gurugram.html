Why Does Back Pain Happen?

Back pain can be caused by a wide range of conditions — from simple muscle strain to complex slip disc, lumbar spondylitis, or sciatica. Most people in Gurugram and Gurgaon lead sedentary desk-job lifestyles, which is a major contributing factor.

Common causes include:

  • Slip disc / disc bulge / herniation
  • Lumbar spondylitis (lower back arthritis)
  • Muscle spasm and ligament strain
  • Sciatica (radiating pain down the leg)
  • Poor posture — especially from desk work
  • Osteoporosis in older adults

🚨 When to See a Doctor Immediately

Seek immediate medical attention if back pain comes with numbness in legs, loss of bladder/bowel control, or severe pain after an injury or fall.

Tips to Prevent Back Pain

  1. Maintain correct posture — keep your spine neutral when sitting
  2. Take breaks every 30 minutes if you have a desk job
  3. Sleep on a medium-firm mattress — avoid very soft or very hard beds
  4. Strengthen your core muscles with low-impact exercises
  5. Lift objects correctly — bend your knees, not your back
  6. Stay hydrated — dehydration affects spinal disc health
