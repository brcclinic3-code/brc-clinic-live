🔒 Privacy Policy

How Healthy Being Clinic collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last Updated: April 2025  |  Effective Date: January 2025
This Privacy Policy applies to the website brcclinic.in and all services offered by Healthy Being Clinic – Pain, Spine & Joint Rehab, Gurugram.

1. About This Policy

Healthy Being Clinic – Pain, Spine & Joint Rehab ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, age, gender (when you fill our contact or appointment forms)
  • Health Information: Condition description, symptoms, treatment history (shared voluntarily for appointment purposes)
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent (via cookies and analytics)
  • Communication Records: WhatsApp messages, emails, and call records related to your inquiries

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your appointment requests and inquiries
  • To provide and improve our medical and therapy services
  • To send relevant health information and updates (only if you opt in)
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Health Information Confidentiality

We treat all health-related information shared with us with the strictest confidentiality. Your medical details will never be shared with third parties without your explicit written consent, except as required by law.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (via Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language and theme preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis
  • Google Maps: For displaying our clinic location
  • WhatsApp Business: For appointment communication

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Health records are maintained per applicable Indian medical regulations.

9. Your Rights

Under applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitute acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: ceo@aarogita.com
  • Phone: +91 93116 42999
  • Address: Healthy Being Clinic, 342P Basement, Islampur Village, Sector 38, Gurugram, Haryana – 122001

Note: This website and clinic operate in India and are subject to Indian laws including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and applicable data protection regulations. All disputes are subject to jurisdiction of courts in Gurugram, Haryana.
